About SGSA Construction Pte Ltd

Repair. Renovate.
Restore.

A distinguished Singapore-based construction company delivering high-quality building and renovation solutions. We are committed to setting elevated standards in construction through meticulous workmanship, strategic planning, and dependable project execution.

Who We Are

Building Excellence,
Delivering Trust

With a strong understanding of industry best practices and regulatory compliance in Singapore, our team manages projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Every project is approached with a focus on quality, safety, and long-term value, ensuring outcomes that meet both functional and aesthetic expectations.

At SGSA Construction, we believe excellence lies in the details. From initial planning to final handover, we work collaboratively with clients, consultants, and stakeholders to deliver seamless construction experiences.

Our structured processes, skilled workforce, and hands-on management allow us to maintain consistency, transparency, and efficiency throughout every stage of the project.

Guided by professionalism and a commitment to continuous improvement, SGSA Construction Pte Ltd strives to be a trusted partner recognized for reliability, superior workmanship, and enduring quality.

What We Deliver

Our Core Services

Comprehensive Renovation Works

Tailored renovation solutions executed with precision, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail.

Ceiling & Wall Partition Systems

Professional installation of durable and functional ceiling and partition systems for residential and commercial spaces.

Painting & Finishing Works

High-quality painting services delivering refined finishes that enhance both aesthetics and longevity.

Tile Laying & Surface Works

Expert tile installation with precise alignment and superior workmanship for floors and walls.

Plumbing Services

Reliable plumbing solutions including installation, replacement, and rectification works in compliance with industry standards.

Electrical Works

Safe and efficient electrical installations, rewiring, and maintenance carried out by qualified professionals.

Reinstatement, Hacking & Demolition

Controlled and compliant dismantling, hacking, and reinstatement services ensuring site safety and readiness for subsequent works.

Air Con Service & Installation

Complete air conditioning solutions including installation, servicing, chemical wash, and repair works by certified technicians.

Plastering Works

Professional plastering and skim coat services for smooth wall finishes and surface preparation prior to painting.

Carpentry Works

Custom carpentry solutions including built-in wardrobes, kitchen cabinets, TV consoles, and feature walls with quality materials.

Waterproofing Services

Comprehensive waterproofing solutions for bathrooms, balconies, and roofs to prevent water seepage and structural damage.
